Classic Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,herbs etc.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp softened butter or oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Optional fillings : cheese , herbs , vegetables

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, crack the eggs and beat them lightly with a fork until combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Add vegetables and cheese as per choice.
  3. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs onto the pan , spread them evenly.
  5. As the eggs begin to set, use a spatula to gently lift the edges and allow any uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  6. Cook for another minute or until the omelette is cooked thoroughly.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy your delicious classic omelette!
